# Project Plan: "Solutions" – Client Experience for My Portfolio

## Vision

Transform my personal portfolio into a dual-purpose website.

The website should serve two completely different audiences:

1. **Recruiters**

   * Evaluate my technical skills.
   * View my projects, experience, and resume.
   * Understand my career journey.

2. **Clients**

   * Understand business problems I solve.
   * Explore AI solutions and case studies.
   * Contact me to start a project.

The experience should feel like entering an AI consulting company rather than another portfolio page.

---

# Primary Goal

The purpose of this section is **not** to showcase projects.

The purpose is to convince a potential client that I can solve their business problem using AI and automation.

The success metric is:

> A visitor contacts me regarding a project.
